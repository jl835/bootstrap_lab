Downtown Boston

Great place to walk around

What to Do and See

Boston Common

Explore the park, walk around the garden, and take pictures of statues such as the Great Embrace and Soldiers and Sailors Monument.

Faneuil Hall Marketplace

A mall with a historical look. Inside is the Quincy Market, the place to find something to eat. The market has many restaurants ranging from smoothies to gyros.

Public Garden

Right next to the main Boston Common, the lake in the middle of the garden has swan boats.

Arts Market

Located on Summer Street Plaza, this event is similar to a farmer's market but with artists and their craft.
